TORBA Retail Trade Assistant

Our free mobile app for sales representatives helps you automate orders, boost sales, and seamlessly sync data with your ERP system in real time. It's the perfect solution for manufacturers and distributors looking to streamline sales agents’ workflows and efficiently manage orders on the go.

Get it on Google Play

Here are some key features:

TORBA Retail – Smart Sales Assistant for Wholesale & Distribution. It empowers sales teams to streamline their workflows, improve communication, and boost overall efficiency.

Real-Time Data Exchange

TORBA Retail ensures seamless communication between your company's server and sales representatives' mobile devices, enabling quick and easy order creation at points of sale.

Comprehensive Client and Product Information

Sales representatives have instant access to all essential client and product data. They can interact with clients, take orders, and send them directly to the company server via the internet.

Versatile Document Creation

With TORBA Retail, sales representatives can create various documents, including product orders, store shelf stock reports, and photo reports of completed tasks in stores.

The app is built to support real-time data exchange between the server of your retail organization and mobile devices used by its sales representatives. TORBA Retail provides sales representatives with essential information on customers and products, allowing for quick order processing and effective communication between your team and clients. The logistics team can then process orders promptly to facilitate timely delivery.

Here’s what the user interface looks like

TORBA Retail – Mobile CRM for Sales Agents & Reps. Our tool simplifies the work of sales representatives with their clients. The app allows them to view their client visit schedule, browse the company's product range with item photos, create orders at points of sale, save GPS coordinates when placing an order, track product stock on store shelves, generate photo reports, and synchronize data with the company's server in real-time.

Client list

The sales representative has access to a list of all their clients along with a daily visit schedule. For each client, the following details are available: general information, contact list, and accounts receivable.

Creating a new document

The sales representative has up-to-date information on the stock levels of all product items across all brands in their company’s warehouse. They have access to detailed information about each product, including photos. On this form, they can create a new order, record product stock on store shelves, and generate a photo report.

Daily work summary

On this form, the sales representative can see their work results for the day: how many clients they visited, the total order value and quantity of products ordered (broken down by brand), the remaining product stock on store shelves (broken down by brand), and photo reports by client.

Accounts receivable

Before placing a new order, the sales representative can review the order history and any unpaid invoices for each client. This helps in making informed decisions when creating a new order.

Shelf photo reports in stores

If ensuring your products are properly displayed on your clients' shelves is one of the sales representative’s tasks, our app allows them to create a photo report of the work completed and instantly send it to your company’s server.

Detailed instructions

Our app provides a comprehensive guide for sales representatives on how to use the app, as well as a manual for system administrators on setting up the data exchange mechanism between your company’s server and the mobile devices of your sales team.

TORBA Retail is a free mobile application for sales agents that eliminates implementation challenges, reduces IT infrastructure costs, and provides businesses with greater flexibility in choosing their tools. It comes with a test database where you can explore its features, create new documents, experiment with setting up data exchange between the mobile app and your company’s server, and more. If you have ideas for enhancing the app’s functionality, please feel free to share them with us, and we’ll consider adding them in future updates.
